Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What are your shipping rates?

Our shipping rates vary based on the delivery location and the size/weight of your order. You can calculate the exact shipping cost at checkout before completing your purchase.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website under "Order Tracking" or directly through the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date for most products. Items must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Some products may have different return policies, which will be noted on the product page.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select countries internationally. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can see if your country is eligible during the checkout process.
