Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I create a business account?

To create a business account, visit our business portal and complete the registration form. You'll need to provide your business details, including your business registration number. Once verified, your account will be activated within 1-2 business days.

What are the minimum quantities for wholesale orders?

Minimum order quantities vary by product category. Most products require a minimum of 10 units, but some specialty items may have higher minimums. You can view specific minimum quantities on each product page when logged into a business account.

Do you offer international shipping for retail customers?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can see if your country is eligible during the checkout process.

Can I return products purchased through the wholesale program?

Yes, we accept returns for wholesale purchases within 14 days of delivery. Please note that custom orders or products with special pricing arrangements may have different return policies, which will be specified at the time of purchase.

How does the personal shopping service work?

Our personal shopping service begins with a brief questionnaire about your preferences. Based on your responses, our team will curate a selection of products that match your style, needs, and budget. You'll receive personalized recommendations via email within 48 hours.
